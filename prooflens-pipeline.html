The verdict

Fuse all signals → Score 0–100 → Band it → Written back to LeadSquared

Doubtful & Suspect are sent to a review log — nothing is ever blocked.

Clear 67–100 Doubtful 34–66 Suspect 0–33
What costs a token? Steps 1–4 and 6 are deterministic and run free on our server. Only step 5’s “right scene?” judgment spends a token — and only on photos that survive the free gates: about 1,500 input + 150 output tokens, ~$0.002 (~₹0.2) per photo, ~$20 per 10,000 — before prompt-caching and the Batch API bring it lower.
ProofLens scores and flags — it never blocks an upload. It raises the cost of faking and catches the lazy majority; it cannot prove a meeting happened.
